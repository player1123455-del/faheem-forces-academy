About the Academy

Faheem Forces Academy

Faheem Forces Academy is dedicated to guiding students through every stage of Pakistan's armed forces entry process — from written tests and ISSB to the interview and medical stage. Our resources are organised by force, so every aspirant finds exactly the material relevant to their chosen path.

We combine self-study material (books & documents) with structured video lectures, so students can learn at their own pace, wherever they are in Pakistan.
